## Question 1 – Rhetorical Triangle

### indentation of:
1. **Speaker**: High executive in the company*
2. **audience**: The customers in general with a bigger focus on the **loyal ones**
3. **purpose**: Announce a price increase of 15%

### Explain how to balance:

balance == hard due to the sensitivity of the subject and how it could affect the customer relation
1. **Logos** (Logic): U need to provide a clear reason for the increase 
2. **Pathos**: (Emotions): u wanna acknowledge the partnership emphasizing the sense of mutual growth
3. **Ethos** (credibility): In this case == Transparency, by facing the audience with the need to increase instead of hiding them in the bill or letting them discover by them selves.

### Identifying 1 ==rhetorical mistake==that could damage credibility and explain why:

**lack of Transparency** which damages ==credibility== 

##### The Reason:
This triggers many feeling from the client side:
1. That they are trapped in the company echo-system and no alternative exists => grows resistance and pushes them to fight back 
2. That the company view them as money machines and not as 2 bodies sharing mutual benefits.
