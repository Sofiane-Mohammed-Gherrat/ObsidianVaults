# Lesson 5 — Separation of Political Power and Corporate Control

### Problem

In the SRC case, the same individual held:

- political leadership
    
- financial authority
    
- advisory role in the company

### Improvement

Government officials should not hold operational roles in government-linked companies.

- Independent professional management
    
- Clear separation between government policy and company operations
