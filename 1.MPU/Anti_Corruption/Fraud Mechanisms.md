# How the SRC International Fraud Mechanism Worked

The SRC International case involved **layering money through multiple companies** to hide the origin of public funds before transferring them to Najib’s personal bank accounts.

## 1. Public Funds Enter SRC International

SRC International received **RM4 billion in government-backed loans** from Malaysia’s pension fund (KWAP). This money was supposed to be used for investments. ([CNA](https://www.todayonline.com/world/najibs-s14m-trial-spider-web-money-transfers-involving-his-personal-accounts?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Najib's S$14m trial: Spider web of money transfers involving his personal accounts - TODAY"))

However, instead of being used for legitimate projects, parts of these funds were **moved out through internal transfers and shell companies**.

---

## 2. Money Transferred to a Subsidiary Company

About **RM50 million** was transferred from SRC International to its subsidiary:

- **Gandingan Mentari Sdn Bhd**
    

This was the **first layer in the money trail**. ([CNA](https://www.todayonline.com/world/asia/mystery-src-international-millions-transferred-najibs-accounts?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Mystery of SRC International, millions transferred into Najib’s accounts - TODAY"))

---

## 3. Transfer to Another Company (Transit Account)

The same funds were then transferred to:

- **Ihsan Perdana Sdn Bhd**
    

This company handled corporate social responsibility (CSR) programmes for 1MDB. ([CNA](https://www.todayonline.com/world/asia/mystery-src-international-millions-transferred-najibs-accounts?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Mystery of SRC International, millions transferred into Najib’s accounts - TODAY"))

Investigators argued that this step was used as a **“transit” or “layering” stage to disguise the money’s origin**. ([Yahoo News Malaysia](https://malaysia.news.yahoo.com/rm42m-transfer-src-misappropriation-najib-104418305.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "RM42m transfer from SRC was misappropriation by Najib as he was sole benefactor, appellate court told - Yahoo News"))

---

## 4. Money Sent to Najib’s Personal Accounts

From Ihsan Perdana, **RM42 million** was transferred into Najib’s personal accounts at **AmBank** in three separate transactions:

- RM27 million
    
- RM5 million
    
- RM10 million
    

These transfers occurred between **December 2014 and February 2015**. ([CNA](https://www.todayonline.com/world/asia/mystery-src-international-millions-transferred-najibs-accounts?utm_source=chatgpt.com "Mystery of SRC International, millions transferred into Najib’s accounts - TODAY"))

# Why This Structure Was Used

Investigators argued that the **multiple companies were used intentionally to conceal the origin of the funds**.

This process is commonly known in financial crime as **“layering”**, a step in money laundering where money is moved through several entities to make it harder to trace. ([Yahoo News Malaysia](https://malaysia.news.yahoo.com/rm42m-transfer-src-misappropriation-najib-104418305.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com "RM42m transfer from SRC was misappropriation by Najib as he was sole benefactor, appellate court told - Yahoo News"))

In court, prosecutors argued there was **no legitimate business reason** for the money to pass through these intermediary companies before reaching Najib’s accounts.
