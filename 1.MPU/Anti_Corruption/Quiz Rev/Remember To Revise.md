### What is fraud

> <mark class="hltr-myyellow">Fraud</mark> is defined as the crime of obtaining money or property by deceiving people.

> <mark class="hltr-mygreen2">deceiving people</mark> means using <mark class="hltr-myyellow">trickery</mark>, <mark class="hltr-myyellow">lies</mark>, or <mark class="hltr-myyellow">misrepresentation</mark> to make someone believe something that isn't true, so they will give up their money, property, or legal rights.
