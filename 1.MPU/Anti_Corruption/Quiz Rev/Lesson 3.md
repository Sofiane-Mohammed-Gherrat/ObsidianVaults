All monitor MACC:
?
1. ==Anti-Corruption Advisory Board (ACAB)==::Advises on the MACC’s <mark class="hltr-myyellow">overall performance</mark>, integrity, and general policies.
2. ==Special Committee on Corruption (SCC)==::A <mark class="hltr-myyellow">group of Parliament</mark> members who <mark class="hltr-mygreen2">review</mark> the MACC's <mark class="hltr-mygreen2">annual reports</mark> and advise the Prime Minister.

5. ==Corruption Prevention & Consultation Panel (CCPP)==::Specifically <mark class="hltr-myyellow">advises on policies</mark> and strategies to **prevent** corruption and gain community support.
