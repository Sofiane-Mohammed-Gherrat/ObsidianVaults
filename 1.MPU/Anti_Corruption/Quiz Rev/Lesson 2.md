# 💼 6. HIGH-RISK JOB AREAS 2:


1.  **Contract & Tender Management:** High risk because employees deal with contractors and suppliers who may use "any means possible" to win a project

2.  **Purchasing & Supply Management:** Similar to contracts, the risk involves abusing power for personal or family gain, such as through **excessive purchases** or **marked-up prices** 

3.  **Enforcement & Regulation:** Risky because of the power to take action against violators—or the **discretion** to simply let them go 

4.  **Office Property & Asset Management:** Vulnerable to the **misuse of assets** for personal benefit or the manipulation of records to hide such misuse

Basically, anywhere someone has the power to "make or break" a deal or an investigation, the risk of corruption goes up! 📈
