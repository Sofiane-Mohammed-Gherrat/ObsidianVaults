# 🌍 6. TRANSPARENCY INTERNATIONAL (TI)

> (TI) is an **international non-governmental organization** **headquartered in Berlin, Germany**

### Important Concepts

#### CPI (Corruption Perception Index)

- Measures:
    - **Perception of public sector corruption**
- Scale:
    - **0 = highly corrupt**
    - **100 = very clean**
- Used by:
    - Investors

❗ Does NOT measure:

- Money laundering
- Private sector corruption
- Illicit financial flows

---

#### GCB (Global Corruption Barometer)

- Measures:
    
    - **People’s real experiences with corruption**

👉 Difference:

- **CPI = experts/business perception**
- **GCB = public experience**
